About Us

Near Dekho is your neighborhood's digital heartbeat. We turn every scroll into stroll, connecting locals with the pulse of their community- from the latest shop deals to upcoming events. We're dynamic, real-time platform that brings your entire neighborhood to life in your pocket.

How It Works

  • Discover businesses and events within their chosen radius.
  • Engage with the community through local news and discussions.
  • Access special promotions and deals from nearby stores.

Our Story

Remember when you knew every shop owner on your street? Us too. As the world went digital, we watched local business fade and neighbors become strangers. That's when Near Dekho stepped in. We're not just an app - we're your neighborhood's missing link. In a world where digital often means distant, we're using tech to reconnect you with the beats and treats of your neighborhood. It's time to fall in love with the local again.
